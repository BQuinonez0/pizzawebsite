About 1 Park Bistro

In 2019, 1 Park Bistro opened its doors with one goal in mind: supporting and serving the Rutgers community. We knew that with quality ingredients we would produce superior quality pizzas. That goal and the promise of supporting and serving the Rutgers community is honored to this day. The more you know about our ingredients, the more informed your pizza choices will be. If you have any ingredient questions, check out our Nutritional Information section below.

Nutritional Information

Crust

Unbleached enriched wheat flour, water, sugar, soybean oil, salt, yeast.

Cheese

Part-skim mozzarella cheese (pasteurized milk, cultures, salt, enzymes),

Pizza Sauce

Fresh vine-ripened tomatoes, sunflower oil, sugar, salt, garlic, spices, extra virgin olive oil, citric acid (maintains freshness).
